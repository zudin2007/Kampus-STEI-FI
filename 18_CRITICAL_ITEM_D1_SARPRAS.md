## INSPECTION PROCESS: D1

### PREP (30 Juni - weekend 1-2 Juli)

1. Prepare inspection team (3-4 people):
   - Kepala Sarpras (lead)
   - Admin Sarpras (documentation)
   - 1 IT tech (for equipment)
   - WK2 representative (overview)

2. Prepare tools:
   - Inspection checklist (print copies)
   - Camera/phone (take photos)
   - Notebook + pen
   - Measuring tape (if measuring spaces)

3. Make inspection checklist:
   - Walls: Cracks? Moisture? Paint peeling?
   - Floor: Clean? Stains? Tiles broken? Slippery?
   - Ceiling: Intact? Water damage? Sagging?
   - AC: Runs? Cold? Leaking? Noise?
   - Lighting: All working? Dim areas?
   - Furniture: Desks OK? Chairs usable? Cabinets secure?
   - Security: Locks work? Windows intact? Doors close?
   - Access: Stairs safe? Ramp available? Disabled access?
   - Cleaning: Trash? Dust? Spills?
   - Special notes: Any unusual observations?

## FINDINGS DOCUMENTATION

For each finding, document:
1. **Location:** Ruang 104, dinding timur
2. **Issue:** Cat mengelupas, area 2m x 1m
3. **Severity:** 
   - 🔴 URGENT (safety/health risk) - AC tidak bekerja, toilet tidak flush
   - 🟡 IMPORTANT (impacts operations) - Proyektor error, lampu mati
   - 🟢 MAINTENANCE (can wait) - Paint touch-up, minor furniture repair
4. **Photo:** Take 2-3 angles
5. **Estimate:** What's needed to fix?
   - Cleaning? (DIY/professional)
   - Repair? (contractor estimate)
   - Replacement? (cost estimate)
   - Maintenance? (service call)
