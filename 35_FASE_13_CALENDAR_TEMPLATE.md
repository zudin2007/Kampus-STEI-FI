## SECTION 4: HOW TO USE THIS TEMPLATE

### Step 1: Compile Dates
From all phases, compile all critical dates into master list:
- From Phase 2: Class schedule start/end
- From Phase 8: Assessment dates
- From Phase 13: Holidays & breaks
- University-wide events: Yudisium, graduation

### Step 2: Verify No Conflicts
☐ No exam same time as holiday
☐ No assessment overload any week
☐ Grade entry deadlines realistic
☐ Assessment dates match rubrics

### Step 3: Choose Format
☐ Month-by-month (for print)
☐ Table/list (for email & portal)
☐ Critical dates summary (for quick reference)
→ Can distribute all 3 formats

### Step 4: Get Approvals
☐ WK1 review (1 day)
☐ Kaprodi review (optional, 1 day)
☐ Dekan approval (1 day)
☐ Rektorat approval (if required, 1-2 days)

### Step 5: Produce Final
☐ Create PDF (high quality)
☐ Print 300 copies
☐ Digital version for portal
☐ Email version for distribution
