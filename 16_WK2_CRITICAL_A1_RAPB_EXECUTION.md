## WHY THIS IS CRITICAL

RAPB (Rencana Anggaran & Pendapatan Belanja) adalah tulang punggung operasional seluruh institusi:
- Tanpa RAPB yang jelas → tidak ada cash flow planning → gaji terlambat → demo dosen & karyawan
- Tanpa alokasi yang tepat → beberapa unit kehabisan dana → sarpras maintenance tertunda
- Tanpa approval Ketua/Dekan → legitimasi operasional lemah → OJK/BAN-PT akan pertanyakan

**Historical risk:** TA 2024 RAPB approval 20 Juli → gaji agustus terlambat 2 minggu → reputasi rusak

## WHAT NEEDS TO HAPPEN

### Phase 1: Reconciliation (1-2 Juli)
**Goal:** Tahu berapa uang masuk & keluar TA 2025 yang sudah berakhir

**Step-by-step:**
1. Pull laporan keuangan final TA 2025 dari sistem (software accounting)
2. Reconcile dengan bank statement Sept 2025 - Agustus 2026
3. Buat daftar outstanding payments (belum dibayar, tapi sudah masuk tagihan)
4. Identify unusual items (overpayment, missing invoices, lainnya)
5. Sign off reconciliation report

**Output:**
- Rekonsiliasi statement: closing balance Sept 2025 = opening balance 2026/2027
- Daftar outstanding items (if any) dengan resolusi
- Memo untuk Ketua: "Cash position saat ini Rp XXX dengan outstanding liabilities Rp YYY"

**Red flag to watch:** Jika ada selisih >5% antara system dan bank → audit lebih dalam sebelum lanjut

### Phase 3: Scenario Planning (6 Juli)
**Goal:** Siapkan plan B jika revenue kurang atau unexpected expense

**Scenarios:**

**Scenario 1: Normal Path (90% revenue realization)**
- Monthly operating budget = Rp XXX M
- Cash flow: positive month 1-3, month 4-9 dapat cash boost dari SPP batch 2
- Reserve untuk emergency: Rp Y M (dalam RAPB as "Dana Cadangan")

**Scenario 2: Downturn Path (70% revenue realization)**
- Immediate actions: cut discretionary spending (training, travel), delay maintenance
- Priority: gaji & honor tetap dibayar (HR risk if not paid)
- Fallback: ambil from Dana Cadangan or request help from Kemenag

**Scenario 3: Upside Path (110% revenue realization)**
- Allocate excess to: sarpras upgrade, dosen research support, student welfare

**Decision rule:** Jika revenue forecast turun >15% by mid-semester → hold all discretionary spending & notify Ketua

## HOW TO AVOID MISTAKES

### ❌ Common Errors:
1. **Overfitting:** Budget terlalu tight, tidak ada buffer → jadi tidak sustainable when unexpected costs arise
   - FIX: Always build in 10-15% contingency buffer
2. **Gaming revenue:** Estimate revenue terlalu optimistic → kenyataan 30% lebih rendah
   - FIX: Use conservative estimate (85% of historical average) not "what we hope for"
3. **Forgetting obligations:** Lupa komitmen (beasiswa, honor dosen kontrak) → cash shortage
   - FIX: First allocate all FIXED commitments, then distribute discretionary
4. **No contingency plan:** Jika ada shock (dosen resign unexpectedly, AC rusak) → panic mode
   - FIX: Write out 3 scenarios (normal, down, up) before approval
