# CRITICAL GUIDE: PKM IMPACT MEASUREMENT

**Owner:** Kepala LPPM  

---

## FOCUS

PKM should have measurable community benefit.

**Questions to ask:**
- Who benefited?
- How many people?
- What changed?
- Sustainable?

---

## MEASUREMENT FRAMEWORK

1. **Community feedback** (survey, interview)
2. **Behavioral change** (evidence of adoption)
3. **Economic impact** (if applicable)
4. **Sustainability** (can benefits continue after PKM ends?)
