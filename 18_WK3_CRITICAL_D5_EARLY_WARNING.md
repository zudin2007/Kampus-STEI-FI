## WHY THIS MATTERS

Early warning system identifies at-risk students BEFORE they drop out.

**If no system:**
- Mahasiswa struggling in silence → eventually drop
- Institution loses tuition revenue
- Akreditasi: retention rate drops → BAN-PT scores down
- Reputation: "students drop easily" → enrollment affects next year

---

## WHAT THE SYSTEM DOES

Monitors these indicators:
- Attendance (<75%)
- GPA (<2.0)
- Incomplete assignments
- No engagement in class discussion
- Financial issues (overdue tuition)

**When triggered:**
- Student flagged as HIGH/MEDIUM/LOW risk
- Intervention plan activated: counseling, tutoring, payment plan, etc
- Follow-up in 2 weeks to verify improvement
