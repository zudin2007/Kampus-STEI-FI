## PURPOSE

Tracer study = track where alumni work + satisfaction with education.

Used for:
- Curriculum improvement (what skills alumni wish they had?)
- Accreditation evidence (BAN-PT wants tracer data)
- Alumni engagement (reconnect with graduates)
- Industry partnerships (identify employer feedback)

1. **Alumni survey** (online, 5-10 min)

   - What should we improve?

2. **Data analysis** (end of July)
   - # responses
   - Employment rate (# alumni employed)
   - Satisfaction score
   - Key feedback themes

3. **Report to WK1** (31 Juli)
   - Summary of findings
   - Recommendations for curriculum
