# FASE 10: WEEKLY MONITORING TEMPLATE & CHECKLIST
## Dosen & ABK Attendance Tracking System

**Applies to:** 1 Agustus - End of Semester (16 weeks)  
**Frequency:** Weekly (Every Monday 9:00 AM report)  
**Owner:** Academic Admin + Kaprodi  
**PIC Report:** Submitted to WK1

---

## OVERVIEW & PURPOSE

This template is used EVERY WEEK to track:
1. **Dosen attendance** - Who's present, who's on leave, any absences
2. **ABK workload** - Is admin/support staff overwhelmed?
3. **Red flags** - Early warning for potential problems
4. **Class coverage** - Are all classes covered by a lecturer?
5. **Action items** - What needs to be fixed this week?

## HOW TO USE THIS TEMPLATE

1. **Save one copy** per week (e.g., "Week1_Monitoring_Report.md")
2. **Fill in sections** 1-4 every Monday morning (takes ~30 min)
3. **Send email report** (Section 5) to Kaprodi by 9:00 AM
4. **Deep-dive** (Section 6) every 2 weeks
5. **Review** mid-semester (Week 8) and end-of-semester (Week 16)
6. **Archive** all completed reports for future reference

**Success metric:** Zero preventable issues, 95%+ dosen attendance, 100% class coverage
