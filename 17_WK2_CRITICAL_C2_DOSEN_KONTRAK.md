## WHY THIS IS CRITICAL

Dosen kontrak adalah 30-40% dari pengajar institusi. Jika kontrak tidak selesai by 14 Juli:
- Honorarium tidak bisa dibayar Sept (dosen tidak punya SK sebagai acuan gaji)
- Kualitas pengajaran menurun (dosen tidak yakin akan diperpanjang → kurang serius)
- Reputasi jelek di pasar tenaga kerja akademis → calon dosen terbaik pergi ke kompetitor
- PMB terpengaruh (calon mahasiswa dengar "dosen sering ganti" → enroll ke universitas lain)

**Historical issue:** TA 2024, SK dosen kontrak baru keluar 30 Sept → gaji pembayaran mundur sampai Nov

## WHAT NEEDS TO HAPPEN

### Phase 1: Database & Sourcing (1-3 Juli)

**Goal:** Tahu berapa dosen kontrak yang butuh direkrut & siapa kandidatnya

**Steps:**

1. **Pull existing dosen kontrak list dari TA 2025**
   - Yang masih willing berlanjut? (survey mereka)
   - Yang sudah resign atau tidak perpanjang? (identify replacement needed)
   
2. **Identify need per program**
   - PRODI ES: butuh X dosen (breakdown per bidang studi)
   - PRODI PS: butuh Y dosen
   - Total: Z dosen kontrak
   
3. **Source candidates**
   - Internal: from alumni, dari jaringan dosen tetap
   - External: job posting di LinkedIn, dari LPTK lain, fresh graduate with promise
   - Target: untuk setiap posisi, identifikasi 2-3 candidates (untuk backup)

**Output:** Spreadsheet daftar calon dengan CV, NIDN (if any), contact, status respons

**Red flag:** Jika for a critical MK (math, physics, etc) tidak ada candidates → ESCALATE SAME DAY ke WK1

---

### Phase 2: Interview & Negotiation (4-8 Juli)

**Goal:** Agree dengan candidates sebelum SK diterbitkan

**Process:**

1. **First screening**
   - CV check (pendidikan, pengalaman, reference)
   - Phone interview (10 min): motivation, availability, exp level
   - Assess: apakah fit untuk posisi?

2. **Second round (for finalists)**
   - Teaching demo (if applicable): 15 min mock class to assess pedagogy
   - Deep interview: background, expectations, availability
   - Reference check (if time permits)

3. **Negotiation**
   - Offer letter: MK yang akan diajar, jam per minggu, honorarium per bulan
   - Clarify: apa ada travel? apa boleh mengajar di temp lain? bagaimana benefit (asuransi, dll)?
   - Get verbal agreement BEFORE paperwork

**Timeline:**
- 4 Juli: first screening done
- 5-7 Juli: second round
- 8 Juli: all offers sent (candidates respond)

**Red flag:** Jika ada candidate yang minta honor lebih tinggi dari budget → negotiate or replace

## HOW TO AVOID MISTAKES

### ❌ Common Errors:

1. **Incomplete documents**
   - E.g., forgot NIDN, nomor rekening not provided
   - Result: Payroll system can't process → gaji tertunda
   - FIX: Use checklist (see above) & collect BEFORE interview

2. **Wrong MK assignments**
   - E.g., engineer assigned to teach philosophy (not expertise)
   - Result: Student feedback bad → reputation damaged
   - FIX: Have WK1/Kaprodi vet the assignment before contract

3. **Honorarium mismatch**
   - E.g., offer Rp 2M but budget only Rp 1.5M
   - Result: Payroll won't approve → can't pay dosen → conflict
   - FIX: Confirm budget availability BEFORE making offer

4. **Lost contact with candidates**
   - E.g., offered to candidate, they went silent → can't reach
   - Result: 30 Juni, still don't know if they're coming
   - FIX: Set clear response deadline (48 hours) with follow-up

5. **SK typos or legal issue**
   - E.g., wrong NIDN, wrong MK code, grammatical error that voids SK
   - Result: Not legally valid → dosen can claim not obligated → walk away
   - FIX: Have legal/admin review SK BEFORE Ketua signs
