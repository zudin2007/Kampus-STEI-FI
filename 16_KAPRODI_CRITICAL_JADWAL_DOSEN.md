# CRITICAL GUIDE: JADWAL DOSEN OPTIMIZATION

**Owner:** Kaprodi  
**Deadline:** 8 Juli  

---

## GOAL

Create teaching schedule that:
- ✅ No time conflicts (dosen tidak mengajar 2 kelas bersamaan)
- ✅ No room conflicts (ruang tidak double-booked)
- ✅ Fair workload (semua dosen ~12-16 SKS)
- ✅ Reasonable hours (tidak semua pukul 07:00)

---

## HOW TO DO IT WELL

1. **Data gathering**
   - Ruang apa yang available?
   - Dosen preference (jam yang disukai)
   - MK enrollment size (untuk tentukan ruang besar/kecil)

2. **Optimization**
   - Use scheduling software (or manual careful planning)
   - Build schedule iteratively
   - Resolve conflicts as they arise

3. **Validation**
   - Check: no double bookings
   - Check: dosen workload balanced
   - Get dosen confirmation

---

## COMMON MISTAKES

❌ Double-booked rooms  
❌ Dosen teaching 2 classes same time  
❌ All classes jam 7am (instructor overload)
