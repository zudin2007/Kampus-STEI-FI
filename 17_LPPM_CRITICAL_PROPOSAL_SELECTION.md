# CRITICAL GUIDE: PROPOSAL SELECTION - MERIT-BASED PROCESS

**Owner:** Kepala LPPM  

---

## PRINCIPLE

Research proposal selection must be fair, transparent, merit-based.

---

## PROCESS

1. **Clear criteria** (announced in call for proposals)
   - Scientific quality
   - Feasibility
   - Relevance to RIP
   - Budget realism

2. **Evaluation** (by review committee)
   - Each proposal scored independently
   - Multiple reviewers
   - Scores documented

3. **Selection** (transparent)
   - Top-scored approved
   - Borderline discussed
   - Decisions documented
   - Feedback to authors

---

## WHAT NOT TO DO

❌ Political influence  
❌ Favoritism  
❌ Lack of transparency
