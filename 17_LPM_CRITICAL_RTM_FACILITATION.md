# CRITICAL GUIDE: RTM FACILITATION

**Owner:** Kepala LPM + Ketua  
**When:** Early August  

---

## PURPOSE

RTM (Rapat Temu Manajemen) = where AMI findings become action.

**If poorly facilitated:**
- Unit heads get defensive → no accountability → no improvement
- No clear CAP agreement → follow-up stalls

---

## FACILITATION TIPS

1. **Preparation**
   - Clear agenda
   - Send findings to unit heads BEFORE meeting (not surprise)
   - Have evidence ready (interview notes, data)

2. **During meeting**
   - Neutral, non-blaming tone
   - Give unit head chance to respond
   - Agree on specific CAP items & deadlines
   - Document decisions

3. **Follow-up**
   - Send meeting minutes same day
   - Monthly CAP check-ins
   - Track completion
