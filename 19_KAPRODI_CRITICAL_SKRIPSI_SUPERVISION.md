# CRITICAL GUIDE: SKRIPSI/TUGAS AKHIR SUPERVISION & QUALITY

**Owner:** Kaprodi  

---

## FOCUS

Final project/thesis quality = institutional reputation.

**Good thesis:**
- Original research
- Properly supervised
- Clear methodology
- Publishable quality (ideally)

**Bad thesis:**
- Plagiarized
- Unsupervised (dosen missing)
- Weak methodology
- Unsaleable quality

---

## SUPERVISION PROTOCOL

1. **Registration**
   - Dosen assigned as advisor
   - Topic approved by committee
   - Timeline created

2. **Ongoing monitoring**
   - Advisor meets with student min 2x/month
   - Progress tracked
   - Meetings documented

3. **Quality assurance**
   - Seminar presentation (before final defense)
   - External examiner review
   - Defense panel assessment

4. **Grading**
   - Multiple assessors
   - Rubric applied
   - Grade submitted

---

## SUCCESS METRICS

- % of students graduating on time
- Quality of thesis (rubric scores)
- Publication from thesis
