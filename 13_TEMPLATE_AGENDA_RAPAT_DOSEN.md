## SESI 3: PEMBELAJARAN & PENILAIAN (30 menit)

**Pembicara:** [NAMA - Kaprodi/Dosen Senior]  
**Slide:** 11-20  
**Handout:** Page 3-4

### Materi yang Disampaikan:

#### A. LEARNING OUTCOMES FRAMEWORK

```
CPMK (Capaian Pembelajaran Mata Kuliah) DEFINITION:
- Spesifik: Apa yang harus dikuasai mahasiswa
- Measurable: Bagaimana diukur
- Achievable: Realistis dalam 1 semester
- Relevant: Aligned dengan kurikulum & industri
- Time-bound: Tercapai dalam durasi mata kuliah

SETIAP DOSEN HARUS:
1. Define CPMK untuk setiap MK yang diampu
2. Align dengan CLO (Course Learning Outcomes) kurikulum
3. Breakdowm menjadi weekly/session learning outcomes
4. Use taxonomy (Bloom's revised): Remember → Evaluate
5. Document semua dalam RPS
```

#### B. TEACHING METHODS YANG ENCOURAGED

```
🎯 ACTIVE LEARNING:
- Discussion & debate
- Group work & collaboration
- Problem-solving tasks
- Peer teaching & tutoring
- Role plays & simulations

🎯 BLENDED LEARNING:
- E-learning platform for materials
- Flipped classroom (pre-class reading/video)
- Synchronous + asynchronous activities
- Virtual lab / simulation
- Online discussion forums

🎯 PROJECT-BASED LEARNING:
- Real-world problems
- Interdisciplinary projects
- Client-based projects
- Portfolio development
- Capstone projects

🎯 ASSESSMENT STRATEGIES:
- Formative: Quiz, discussion, exercise (frequent feedback)
- Summative: Midterm exam, final exam, project
- Authentic: Portfolio, real-world problem
- Self-assessment & peer review
```

**Best Practices:**
```
✓ Mix berbagai metode (tidak hanya lecture)
✓ Involve mahasiswa sebagai active learner
✓ Clear learning objectives di awal setiap session
✓ Regular feedback (tidak tunggu akhir semester)
✓ Technology-enhanced tapi tetap human-centric
✓ Accessible untuk semua learning styles
```

#### C. GRADING & ASSESSMENT SYSTEM

```
BOBOT PENILAIAN (Standard):
├─ Tugas/Quiz/Exercise: 30% (frequent formative assessment)
├─ UTS (Midterm Exam): 35% (comprehensive)
└─ UAS (Final Exam): 35% (final assessment)

ALTERNATIVE (untuk MK tertentu):
├─ Project-based: Tugas 50%, Exam 50%
├─ Portfolio-based: Portfolio 60%, Exam 40%
├─ Research-based: Paper 70%, Presentation 30%

GRADING SCALE:
A  = 85-100  (4.0 GPA)
B+ = 80-84   (3.5 GPA)
B  = 75-79   (3.0 GPA)
C+ = 70-74   (2.5 GPA)
C  = 65-69   (2.0 GPA) ← MINIMUM COMPETENCY
D  = 60-64   (1.0 GPA)
E  = <60     (0.0 GPA) ← FAIL

MINIMUM COMPETENCY:
- Grade C (2.0) untuk semua MK
- GPA minimum 2.0 per semester
- GPA < 2.0 → Academic Probation → Intervention

ACADEMIC PROBATION PROCESS:
1. Dosen wali → contact mahasiswa & identify issues
2. Student → meet dengan academic advisor
3. Plan → remedial action (retake, tutoring, counseling)
4. Monitoring → check progress tiap 2 minggu
5. Review → semester depan ada improvement?
```

#### D. FEEDBACK STRATEGY

```
FORMATIVE FEEDBACK (DURING SEMESTER):
- Frequency: Regular (at least 2x per month)
- Method: Written (rubric), verbal, email
- Content: Strength & area for improvement
- Timeliness: Return within 1 week
- Actionable: Clear guidance on how to improve

SUMMATIVE FEEDBACK (AFTER EXAM):
- Grade explanation: Why this score?
- Benchmark: How compared to class average?
- Guidance: If failed, how to improve next time?
- Availability: Office hours untuk discussion

TECHNOLOGY-AIDED FEEDBACK:
- E-learning: Automated quiz feedback
- Rubric: Clear scoring criteria
- Voice recording: For detailed feedback
- Comments: Specific examples dari submission
```

## SESI 7: Q&A & DISKUSI (45 menit)

**Format:** Open discussion, capture di flipchart

### Anticipated Questions & Prepared Answers:

```
Q: "Berapa jam maksimal dosen boleh mengajar per semester?"
A: Standar 12-16 jam/minggu dianggap optimal. Maksimal 18-20 
   jam dengan approval Dekan. Beyond itu tidak sustainable untuk 
   quality research & development.

Q: "Kalau ada dosen yang tidak bisa pakai e-learning, bagaimana?"
A: Kami provide training & support. Tidak ada alternative - semua 
   dosen HARUS minimal upload materi di e-learning. Yang kesulitan 
   bisa contact IT untuk assistance.

Q: "Bagaimana jika ada mahasiswa yang tidak terima nilainya?"
A: Ada appeal mechanism: mahasiswa bisa meeting dengan dosen, 
   request re-scoring. Jika masih tidak setuju, escalate ke Kaprodi 
   untuk arbitration. Tapi keputusan dosen generally final setelah 
   rubric dijelaskan.

Q: "Deadline grade submission ketat sekali, apakah bisa extended?"
A: Tidak ada extension tanpa alasan force majeure (sakit parah, 
   keluarga meninggal, dll). Jika ada masalah teknis (sistem error), 
   immediately contact IT. Grade yang delayed akan di-flag & impact 
   dosen performance evaluation.

Q: "Bagaimana dengan student yang consistently absent?"
A: Dosen wali akan intervene. Dosen harus report ke akademik office 
   jika ada mahasiswa dengan absence >25%. Kami punya counseling & 
   support untuk identify root cause (health, personal, financial).

Q: "Apakah ada consequence jika grade submission late?"
A: Ya, performance evaluation & may impact salary adjustment atau 
   teaching load. Jadi please take seriously & plan ahead untuk grading.

Q: "Apakah harus pakai innovative teaching methods atau lecture OK?"
A: Lecture adalah salah satu method yang OK, tapi encourage mix dengan 
   active learning untuk better engagement. Bukan one-size-fits-all - 
   disesuaikan dengan nature of subject & student background.
```

### Open Discussion Topics:

- Challenges dalam implementasi active learning
- Teknologi yang bisa digunakan (yang sudah ada vs yang baru)
- Work-life balance dalam research vs teaching load
- How to support struggling students
- Collaboration opportunities antar dosen
