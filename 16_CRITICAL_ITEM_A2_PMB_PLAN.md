**CRITICAL METRIC:** Minimum 2 kelas per prodi (institusi mandate)

### 7. CONTINGENCY PLANS

**Risk 1: Pendaftar lebih sedikit dari target**
- Early action (Week 2): Monitor daily, if trend bad, escalate
- Mid-course correction: Extra promosi push, target new schools
- Worst case: Relax criteria slightly, prioritize quality orientation

**Risk 2: Platform/system issues**
- Backup: Manual form at office
- Support: Help desk at admin for technical issues

**Risk 3: Market competition** (other programs/schools)
- Point of differentiation: Islamic education focus? Strong alumni outcomes? Affordable fee?
- Value proposition: Include in promosi materials

## A2 RED FLAGS TO AVOID

🚫 Vague target ("kami target banyak pendaftar")  
→ FIX: Specify number per prodi, rationale

🚫 Unrealistic timeline (e.g., "promotion mulai 25 Juli")  
→ FIX: Start earlier (19 Juli latest), allow 2 weeks minimum

🚫 No contingency ("optimis semua akan jalan smooth")  
→ FIX: Include risk scenarios & mitigation

🚫 Unclear cost/payment (students confused about how to pay)  
→ FIX: Step-by-step instruction + FAQ

🚫 No mention of selection criteria (looks ad-hoc)  
→ FIX: Clear academic/character criteria documented
