# FASE 9: PROGRAM PENGENALAN KAMPUS MAHASISWA BARU
## OMB (Orientasi Mahasiswa Baru) - TA 2026/2027

**Timeline:** 1-10 Agustus 2026  
**PIC:** Kemahasiswaan + WK1  
**Owner:** Dekan

---

## QUICK OVERVIEW

**Tujuan:** Kenalkan mahasiswa baru ke kampus, sistem akademik, budaya, & support services sebelum kuliah dimulai

**Output:** Mahasiswa baru paham sistem akademik, tahu support services, siap kuliah

---

## FASE 9 CHECKLIST

### COORDINATION (1-3 AGUSTUS):

```
PLAN:
☐ Decide format: Full-day campus tour? Multi-day? Virtual?
☐ Schedule: [TGL & JAM]
☐ Venue/location: [LOKASI]
☐ Capacity planning: [JUMLAH MAHASISWA]

PREPARE:
☐ Orientation materials (handbook, campus map)
☐ Presentations ready (academic systems, support services)
☐ Assign guides/mentors
☐ IT check: E-learning access, SIAKNG demo
☐ Refreshment arrange
☐ Transportation (if campus is spread out)
```

### CONTENT SESSIONS (3-5 AGUSTUS - Planning Phase):

```
SESSION 1: WELCOME & CAMPUS TOUR (1 hour)
├─ Welcome dari Dekan/Rektorat
├─ Physical campus tour (lab, library, facilities)
└─ Safety & facilities info

SESSION 2: ACADEMIC SYSTEMS (1 hour)
├─ SIAKNG overview (registration, KRS, grades)
├─ E-learning platform demo
├─ Course expectations & academic integrity
└─ Q&A

SESSION 3: SUPPORT SERVICES (45 min)
├─ Student services (counseling, health, financial aid)
├─ Library & IT helpdesk
├─ Clubs & activities
└─ Contact info for each service

SESSION 4: STUDENT LIFE (45 min)
├─ Student handbook & code of conduct
├─ Rights & responsibilities
├─ Complaint mechanism
└─ Campus life expectations

SESSION 5: OPEN Q&A & SOCIAL (45 min)
├─ All questions welcome
├─ Meet dengan dosen wali (light)
└─ Informal networking
```

### EXECUTION (5-10 AGUSTUS):

```
LOGISTICS:
☐ Register all mahasiswa baru
☐ Assign to groups (jika ada multiple sessions)
☐ Send reminder email (2 hari sebelum)
☐ Prepare welcome packets

DAY OF:
☐ Setup ruang (kursi, projector, suara)
☐ Greet students (30 menit early)
☐ Attendance checklist
☐ Collect feedback forms

DOCUMENTATION:
☐ Take photos/video (for memory, social media)
☐ Collect contact info dari peserta
☐ Send thank you email after
```

## POST-OMB FOLLOW-UP:

```
WEEK 1 (After OMB):
☐ Send welcome email dengan all resources & contacts
☐ Post OMB materials on portal/website
☐ Email dari dosen wali: "Let's schedule first meeting"
☐ Verify all mahasiswa registered & have SIAKNG access

IF ISSUE:
- Student missed OMB → One-on-one orientation
- No SIAKNG access → IT support immediately
- Questions about academics → Refer to Kaprodi
```
