## COMMON ISSUES & QUICK FIXES

| Issue | Symptom | Quick Fix | Escalate If |
|-------|---------|-----------|-------------|
| AC not cold | Room temp >27°C | Check thermostat, clean filter | Service needed >24 hrs |
| WiFi weak | <2 bars | Move AP location, reboot | Hardware failure |
| Proyektor blurry | Lens dusty/misaligned | Clean lens, realign | Bulb needs replace |
| Electrical outlet dead | No power to outlet | Check breaker, test circuit | Multiple outlets dead |
| Lab equipment dormant | No power or malfunction | Check power/cables, basic troubleshoot | Requires technician |
