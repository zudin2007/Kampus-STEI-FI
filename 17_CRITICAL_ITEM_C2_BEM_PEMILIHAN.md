# CRITICAL ITEM: C2 PEMILIHAN PENGURUS BEM BARU (IF APPLICABLE)
## Deep-Dive Execution Guide

**Risk Level:** 🔴 CRITICAL (if C1 decision = perlu pemilihan)  
**Dependency:** Blocks C3-C5 if delayed  
**Timing:** Mid-July (tentatif, depends C1 evaluation)  
**Owner:** WK3 (facilitate), BEM (execute)  

---

## WHY C2 IS CRITICAL

**Legitimacy & student leadership continuity.**

Jika C2 tidak dilakukan dengan proper:
- Pengurus baru tidak legitimate → student compliance rendah
- Program kerja tidak punya energy → kegiatan kemahasiswaan hidup-hidup segan
- Akreditasi: BAN-PT akan tanya "Bagaimana governance organisasi mahasiswa anda?"

---

## C2 DECISION TREE: WHEN IS SELECTION NEEDED?

### Scenario A: Perpanjangan Pengurus (No selection needed)
**If:**
- Current BEM performing well (track record, feedback positif)
- Ketua & core team mau continue
- No major issues/scandal

**Action:** Skip C2, go to C3 (rapat koordinasi) langsung
**Timing:** Early July

### Scenario B: Pemilihan Pengurus (Selection process needed)
**If:**
- Current ketua/tim sudah habis masa jabatan (usually 1-2 tahun)
- Performance struggling (low program execution, financial issues)
- Major incident/conflict
- Student demand untuk pergantian

**Action:** Execute C2 fully
**Timing:** Mid-July

---

## C2 EXECUTION: PEMILIHAN PENGURUS BARU

### PHASE 1: PREPARATION (Early July)

**Step 1a: Clarify Timeline & Process**
- When: Kapan voting/musyawarah? (typically mid-Juli)
- Format: Voting or musyawarah? (check BEM AD/ART)
- Candidate requirement: Kriteria kandidat apa? (aktif, tidak pernah skors, akademik OK?)

**Step 1b: Coordinate dengan BEM**
- Current ketua will facilitate the process (mereka yang tau AD/ART)
- WK3 role: Oversee, ensure fair process, legitimize hasil
- Timeline: Current pengurus harus prepare everything → selesai mid-July

**Step 1c: Announce ke mahasiswa**
- Candidates opening: When? How to apply?
- Voting/musyawarah date: Confirm & announce (at least 1 week notice)
- Eligibility: Who can vote? (seluruh mahasiswa? active only?)

### PHASE 2: CANDIDATE NOMINATION (Week 1-2)

**Step 2a: Open call for candidates**
- Medium: Email, BEM announcement, social media
- Info provided: Kriteria, timeline, how to apply
- Deadline: Jelas (e.g., "candidates must submit by 16 July")

**Step 2b: Candidate vetting**
- Who does this? (usually BEM panitia pemilihan)
- Criteria check:
  - ☐ Akademik: IPK min 2.0 atau sesuai requirement
  - ☐ Conduct: Tidak ada record pelanggaran
  - ☐ Commitment: Siap kerja full year
  - ☐ Motivation: Have clear vision for BEM

**Step 2c: Publish candidate list**
- Make list public (transparency)
- Include candidate profile: Year, prodi, vision statement
- Timeline for voting: "Voting on [DATE]"

### PHASE 3: CAMPAIGN (Week 2)

**Optional—some institutions allow candidate campaigns:**
- Time limit: E.g., 3 days before voting
- Method: Poster, social media, in-person presentation
- Rules: Fair, respectful, no personal attacks

### PHASE 4: VOTING/MUSYAWARAH (Mid-July)

**Option A: Democratic Voting**
- Who can vote? (defined in AD/ART)
- Process: Online atau offline? (consider accessibility)
- Voting booth: Ensure privacy & security
- Monitoring: WK3 or independent observer supervise
- Counting: Transparent counting process

**Option B: Musyawarah (Consensus)**
- Facilitator: Usually senior BEM or WK3
- Process: Discussion → agree on candidates
- Documentation: Catat who agreed & decision rationale

**Keputusan:**
- Winner: Ketua & core positions
- SK preparation: WK3 draft SK pengurus baru

### PHASE 5: SK & INSTALLATION (Late July)

**Step 5a: Finalize SK**
- Format: Official letterhead, numbered
- Content: Nama-nama pengurus, jabatan, berlaku mulai [DATE]
- Sign by: WK3 (if authorized) or Ketua/Dekan

**Step 5b: Installation Ceremony (optional)**
- Saat: Bisa langsung atau ceremonial event
- Involve: Dosen pembina, senior BEM, students
- Message: Welcome new leadership, set expectations

**Step 5c: Briefing Pengurus Baru**
- Topics: Tupoksi (job description), program kerja, budget process, accountability
- Timing: Day 1 après pemilihan
- Owner: WK3 + Dosen pembina

## C2 BEST PRACTICES

### Ensure Fairness:
✓ Transparent criteria & timeline publicly announced  
✓ Fair vetting (consistent application of rules)  
✓ Monitored voting (no manipulation)  
✓ Results announced promptly  

### Ensure Legitimacy:
✓ Follow BEM AD/ART (tidak buat proses baru)  
✓ Document everything (minutes, SK, decisions)  
✓ Get stakeholder buy-in (students, dosen pembina, WK3)  
✓ Announce results clearly ("X elected ketua" dll)  

### Ensure Momentum:
✓ Quick transition (pemilihan → briefing → program kerja approval → eksekusi)  
✓ Dukung newly elected (resources, budget, confidence)  
✓ Involve in C3 rapat koordinasi (signal: "you're the leaders now")  

---

## C2 RED FLAGS TO AVOID

🚫 **Vague timeline** ("pemilihan sometime di Juli")  
→ FIX: Announce exact date at least 1 week in advance

🚫 **No criteria** (seems like popularity contest)  
→ FIX: Publish akademik & conduct requirements

🚫 **Opaque voting** (results announced vaguely)  
→ FIX: Public counting, share detailed results

🚫 **No documentation** (hard to verify later)  
→ FIX: Keep minutes, SK, candidate list for record

🚫 **Long gap** (pemilihan → no follow-up untuk weeks)  
→ FIX: Immediate briefing, include dalam C3 rapat minggu berikutnya

---

## C2 CONTINGENCY

**Risk: Candidate withdrawal / Health issue**
→ Schedule runoff vote atau extend nominations

**Risk: Low voter turnout**
→ Extend voting period, encourage participation (e.g., "pemilihan adalah hak & tanggung jawab mahasiswa")

**Risk: Dispute over results**
→ Recount, check records, meet dengan disputing party (audi their concern)

3. **Monitor**: C3-C4 program kerja approval, ensure they deliver
