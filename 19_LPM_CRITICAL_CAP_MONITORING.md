# CRITICAL GUIDE: CAP MONITORING & FOLLOW-UP

**Owner:** Kepala LPM  

---

## WHY MONITORING MATTERS

CAP is only useful if tracked. Without monitoring:
- Findings → recommendations → forgotten
- No actual improvement happens

---

## MONITORING PROCESS

1. **Monthly check-in**
   - Email to unit head: "What progress on your CAP?"
   - Request status update

2. **Verify**
   - Don't just take their word
   - Ask for evidence
   - Spot-check if critical items

3. **Escalate if stalled**
   - If unit not making progress → alert Ketua
   - May need Ketua intervention

4. **Document**
   - Keep tracking spreadsheet
   - For next audit cycle & accreditation

---

## TRACKING METRICS

- # CAP items completed on time
- # CAP items delayed
- % of planned improvements realized
