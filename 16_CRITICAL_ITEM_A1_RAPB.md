# CRITICAL ITEM DEEP DIVE: A1 RAPB CONFIRMATION
## Why This Matters & What To Do

---

## THE PROBLEM

RAPB (Rencana Anggaran Pendapatan & Belanja) is the **master budget for entire year**.

Without RAPB confirm by 1 Juli:
- ❌ Admin Keuangan can't allocate operational budget (A3)
- ❌ Admin Umum can't plan kegiatan awal semester (A5)
- ❌ SDM can't finalize pembayaran gaji/honor (C4)
- ❌ Sarpras can't plan perbaikan urgent (D2)
- ❌ WK1, WK3, WK4 can't plan academic/kemahasiswaan/research budget
- **Result:** ENTIRE PREPARATION STALLS

---

## WHAT IS RAPB?

RAPB = Institutional budget document showing:
- Total revenue (expected tuition, grants, other)
- Total expenses (by category)
- Allocation per unit/department
- Cash flow projection

Must be:
- ✅ Approved by Yayasan Board
- ✅ Signed by Rektorat
- ✅ Legally binding for fiscal year

## WHAT IF RAPB IS NOT READY?

### Contingency Plan:

**Scenario 1:** RAPB exists but not signed yet
- Action: Follow up Rektorat/Yayasan daily
- Timeline: Target sign within 2-3 days
- Interim: Ask for interim budget allocation (80% of prior year) to keep work moving

**Scenario 2:** RAPB is being revised (last minute changes)
- Action: Ask: When will it be ready?
- If > 1 week delay: Escalate to Ketua
- Risk: ALL phases delayed

**Scenario 3:** RAPB doesn't exist yet (!!)
- Action: IMMEDIATE escalation to Rektorat
- This means budget process was NOT done on time
- Institutional crisis — needs rector-level intervention
