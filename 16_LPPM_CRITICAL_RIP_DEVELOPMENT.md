## PURPOSE

RIP (Rencana Induk Penelitian) = long-term (4 year) research strategy for the institution.

**Why critical:** Sets direction for all faculty research efforts

---

## COMPONENTS

1. **Research vision** - Where do we want to be in 4 years?
2. **Priority themes** - Which topics are strategic?
3. **Resource plan** - Budget, facilities, staffing
4. **Action plan** - How to move from vision to reality

---

## SUCCESS CRITERIA

✅ All stakeholders agree on priorities  
✅ Budget realistic & confirmed  
✅ Timeline clear
