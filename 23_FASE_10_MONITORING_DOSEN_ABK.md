## OVERVIEW

**Tujuan:**
- ✅ Track kehadiran dosen setiap minggu

## B. CONTINGENCY PLAN - DOSEN SUBSTITUTION

### If Dosen Unavailable:

```
PRIORITY 1: SAME SUBJECT EXPERT
└─ Who: Senior dosen di area yang sama
├─ Contact: Email + phone call
├─ Timeline: Inform within 2 hours
└─ Approval: Kaprodi sign-off required

PRIORITY 2: NEARBY SUBJECT OVERLAP
└─ Who: Dosen dengan expertise related
├─ Modification: Adjust materi (if needed) untuk stay relevant
├─ Timeline: Inform same day
└─ Approval: Kaprodi + dosen agreement

PRIORITY 3: CLASS RESCHEDULE
└─ If no substitute available
├─ Reschedule untuk: [NEXT AVAILABLE DATE]
├─ Inform students: Email + announcement
└─ Make-up: Add to end of semester

NEVER:
❌ Cancel class tanpa student notification
❌ Reschedule tanpa dokumentasi
❌ Leave class unsupervised
```

## D. EARLY WARNING SYSTEM

### Red Flags:

```
🚨 IMMEDIATE ESCALATION (Same day):
  1. Dosen multiple no-shows
  2. Lab closure due to staff shortage
  3. System (SIAKNG) down
  4. ABK staff resignation
  5. Safety incident

⚠️  MONITOR CLOSELY (Check next week):
  6. Dosen frequent sick days (>2x/month)
  7. Student complaints about teaching quality
  8. Document processing backlog >1 week
  9. Lab equipment broken & no timeline to fix
  10. ABK staff requesting leaves

📋 ROUTINE (Regular check):
  11. Schedule adjustments needed
  12. Dosen workload imbalance (some overload, others light)
  13. ABK cross-training gaps
  14. Facility maintenance deferred
```
